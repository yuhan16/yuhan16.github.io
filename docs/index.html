About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate at in Electrical and Computer Engineering at New York University, working under the guidance of Prof. Quanyan Zhu. My research centers on the planning and coordination of multi-agent systems, integrating methodologies across game theory, optimization, and machine learning. Previously, I received my master's in Robotics from the University Pennsylvania (GRASP lab) and my bachelor's in Automation from Beijing Institute of Techonology.

Research Interests

  • Dynamic Games and Decision Theory
  • Learning and Optimization
  • Multi-Robot Systems
